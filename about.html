About Me

I'm Phạm Phi Khanh, a Year 1 student majoring in Embedded Systems & IoT at Da Nang University of Technology. I grew up in Ea H'Leo, a rural district in Đắk Lắk province, where access to technology was limited but curiosity was not.

"When billions of devices are connected to the internet, who is protecting them?"

That curiosity led me to a question that now drives everything I do. Beyond engineering, I've spent a year running interdisciplinary research applying AI to real community problems, an experience that taught me as much about how I work with others as it did about machine learning.

This portfolio isn't just a record of what I've done. It's an honest reflection of who I am, what I've learned about myself, and where I'm headed.
